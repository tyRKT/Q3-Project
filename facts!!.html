Facts about Beabadoobee
1. She grew up in West London listening to original Pinoy music as well as pop and rock music from the 1980s.
2. She was educated at Sacred Heart High School before completing her thirteenth year at Hammersmith Academy.
3. Beabadoobee has cited Elliott Smith, Mac DeMarco, the Moldy Peaches, Pavement, Mazzy Star, the Beatles, Simon and Garfunkel, and Daniel Johnston as her musical influences.
4. She has a tattoo of Johnston’s artwork from the cover of his album Continued Story with Texas Instruments.
5. Laus spent seven years learning to play the violin, before getting her first guitar second-hand at the age of 17.
6. She was nominated for the Rising Star Award at the 2020 Brit Awards
7. At age 15, Laus began dating British film director Soren Harrison who is known for his works with The 1975 and Måneskin
